Hey, John Sonmez here...

I want to personally thank you for subscribing to Simple Programmer and tell you a little bit about what you can expect from me and these emails.

You probably signed up for Simple Programmer because you either read one of my blog posts or wanted to find out more information about one of my products.

Either way, I am glad that you decided to join the Simple Programmer community. I reserve some of my best content for subscribers to this list.

Usually you can expect to get two emails from me each week—unless you sign up for one of my email courses (like 5 Learning Mistakes Software Developers Make or How to Create a Blog that Boosts Your Career).

Most Mondays I send a short email that highlights the best content on Simple Programmer. Sometimes this will be a single post, and other times you might get a roundup of everything I'm up to.

I also send out an email at the end of the week that is usually a little more personal in nature. You never know what you'll get from one of these emails, but I try to keep them entertaining as well as informative.

Occasionally (maybe once every 2-3 months) I may also send you a couple of extra emails during the week when I have a special offer that might benefit you.

Well, thanks again for joining and I look forward to hearing from you and serving you as best I can. If there is ever anything I can do for you please do not hesitate to reach out to me.
